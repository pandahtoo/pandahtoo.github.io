BIOGRAPHY

Brandon Paul Brian Williams (born 3 September 2000) is an English
professional footballer who plays as a full-back for EFL Championship club Ipswich Town, on loan from Manchester United. Although he mainly plays as a left-back, he has also been used as a right-back.[4] Williams is a graduate of the Manchester United youth academy and made his first-team debut in September 2019. He has represented England at under-20 and under-21 levels.
